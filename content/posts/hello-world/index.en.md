# Hello World

Welcome to my personal website! This site is built with [az-site](https://github.com/arthurzhuhan/az-site), an AI-native personal website template.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Blog** — My thoughts on technology, building, and life
- **Resources** — Tools, projects, and learning materials I recommend
- **AI Chat** — Chat with my AI assistant to learn more about me

## Getting Started

This is an example post that comes with the template. Feel free to delete it and start writing your own content!

To create a new post, add a folder under `content/posts/` with `index.en.md` and `index.zh.md` files.
